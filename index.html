HAMPI

Hampi is located in the northern part of the Indian state of Karnataka. Renowned for its captivating ruins, Hampi was the last capital of the Vijayanagar Empire during the 14th to 16th centuries

TAJ MAHAL

The Taj Mahal in Uttar Pradesh, is a symbol of India's rich cultural heritage and has inspired various works of art, literature, and poetry. It stands as a testament to love and craftsmanship, drawing admiration for its beauty and historical significance.

AJANTA

Ajanta Caves in Maharashtra, feature 30 ancient Buddhist art caves from the 2nd century BCE, illustrating the evolution of Indian art with intricate paintings and sculptures.
